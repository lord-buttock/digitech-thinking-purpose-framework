ICT User Agreement – Foundation to Year 2

General use
1. I will keep the iPad in its case when I’m not using it.
2. I will not eat or drink near an iPad.
3. I will tell my teacher if anything bad happens to the iPad.

Content
4. I understand that there will be consequences for inappropriate use.
5. I will use the iPad for education purposes unless my teacher informs me otherwise.

Safety & Security
6. When using the Internet/School Intranet/Applications at School:
• I will only use the Internet when asked by my teacher.
• I will never send a person my photograph without first checking with my teacher.
• I will ask my teacher before sending a message.
• I will tell my teacher if anything unusual happens with the iPad.
• I will only take an iPad outside if my teacher tells me to.
• I will not take photos or record video of other people without asking them.
7. I am responsible for looking after the iPad.
8. I will use the iPad and other ICT equipment correctly with regard to other people to maintain a neat
and tidy workspace.

Consequences
9. I understand if the above conditions are not followed, my parents will be contacted, and this may
lead to:
• a warning
• not being allowed to use an iPad at school
