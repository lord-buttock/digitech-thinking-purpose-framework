ICT User Agreement – Years 3 to 6

General use
1. I will bring my iPad to school each day, fully charged and ready for use.
2. I will keep my iPad in its case when transporting it around the school or at home.
3. I will store my iPad in a safe location. For example, I will not leave the device on the floor.
4. I will keep food and drinks away from my iPad at school and at home.
5. I will immediately report any accidents or breakages to parents and teachers.
6. I understand that, at school, my iPad is primarily to support my education.

Content
7. I understand my teachers and parents will perform checks to monitor that I have not installed illegal
programs/unsuitable
applications and content, and will check my website browsing history.
8. I understand that there will be consequences for inappropriate use.
9. I will only use my iPad for non-educational purposes with the explicit permission of a member of staff.

Safety & Security
10. I will use the Internet to support my learning, employing cybersafe and cybersmart practices.
11. When using the Internet/School Intranet/Applications/Email at School:
• I will only work on the Internet for purposes specified by my teacher or adult in charge.
• I will not give out any personal information, such as surname, addresses, telephone numbers, and
parent/guardian work addresses/telephone numbers.
• I will never send a person my photograph without first checking with my teacher/parent/carer.
• I will always obtain teacher/parent/carer permission before sending an email/message.
• I will compose email messages only using language and images I understand are acceptable in my school.
• I will not respond to any messages that are unknown, unpleasant or that make me feel uncomfortable in any
way and I will inform my teacher/parent/carer immediately if I receive these messages. Evidence of inappropriate
material will be kept.
• I will not use material from Internet sites unless I have permission from the person who created the material or I
reference where the material was obtained. If I am unsure, I will check with my teacher or adult in charge.
• I will not use my iPad outside unless directed by a teacher.
• I will not take/record nor publish photos or videos of other people without their express permission.
• I will not use the Internet/Applications, e.g., social media, to frighten, annoy, harass, or offend another person.
• I will follow school guidelines and procedures when preparing materials for publication on the Internet or
printing.
12. I will use my iPad and other ICT equipment correctly with regard to other people to maintain a neat and tidy
workspace.
13. I will use the iPad lawfully and in accordance with the ICT Acceptable Use Policy and Student Wellbeing and
Engagement

Policies and Guidelines
14. For security reasons, I am not to share account names and passwords with anyone unless requested by staff
or technicians when servicing the iPad.
15. I am responsible for looking after my iPad.
16. If the memory space on my iPad nears capacity and compromises the use of my iPad at school, my
parents/carers will be contacted and personal apps, photos/movies will need to be removed.
17. If I use my own iPad, I understand that if the above conditions are not followed, I will not be granted the
authorisation to use
my iPad at school.
18. I will not have a VPN on my iPad.

Consequences
19. I understand if the above conditions are not followed, my parents will be contacted and this may lead to:
• the confiscation of my iPad for a period of time
• not being allowed to use my iPad at school for a period of time
• removal of internet access at school for a period of time
• removal of messaging applications, e.g., iMessage, from my device
• if a VPN is present, the iPad will be removed from the school network and reconnection to the network will be by
negotiation with the ICT team and school leadership.
