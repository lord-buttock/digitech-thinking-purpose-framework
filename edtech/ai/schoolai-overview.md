## AI Tools

### SchoolAI
- AI platform for teachers to create lessons, spaces, and activities using AI assistance
- Supports Google, Microsoft, Clever, and Classlink login
- Example use: generate a 30-minute lesson on Earth's layers for Year 5, complete with critical thinking questions
- [Open SchoolAI](https://app.schoolai.com/organize?owner=me&tab=my-creations) ✅ Active

---

### Classroom AI Use: What's Cheating? What's OK?
- A visual spectrum from "More AI Creation" to "More Human Work" — useful for setting AI use expectations with students and staff
- Created by Matt Miller of Ditch That Textbook
- Ranges from "AI does student work with no thought" → to "Student does all work without any assistance from AI"
- Good for classroom discussions, school policy, and parent communication
- [View image](https://blog.tcea.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Screenshot-2024-01-15-at-2.05.46-PM-1536x862.png) ✅ Active
