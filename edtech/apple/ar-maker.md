## AR Maker

AR Maker (ARMakr) is an iPad app for creating augmented reality scenes and objects. Resources here cover a wide range of cross-curricular AR projects saved to Trello in late 2019–early 2020. Most original source links are via Twitter/X. The Moon cycle card has a publicly accessible Google Drive resource.

### Key ideas
- AR Maker integrates powerfully with cross-curricular units: science (moon, scale, planetarium), literacy (characters, author study), maths (protractor, timeline), and creative arts
- The **Moon cycle** Google Drive resource is the only confirmed open-access link
- Works well alongside Keynote and Numbers in Apple-focused units (see the Space lesson card in [[Edtech/Apple/Keynote]])
