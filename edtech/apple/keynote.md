## Keynote

The largest list on the Trello Edtech board (25 cards). Keynote is used far beyond presentations — as an animation studio, interactive learning tool, AR companion, character maker, infographic builder, and reading resource. Most 2019 cards link via Twitter/X; several 2020 cards have descriptive text and open URLs.

### Key ideas
- **Interactive learning**: character maps, guided reading, choose-your-own-adventure, and app prototyping all use Keynote's hyperlink/tap interactions
- **Animation studio**: animated scenes, GIF exports, magnifying reveals, and multi-video windows push Keynote well beyond slides
- **Cross-curricular unit potential**: the Space lesson alone combines Keynote + Numbers + GarageBand + AR Maker
- **50 Days of Keynote**: a community challenge series — the Padlet is the best open-access archive
