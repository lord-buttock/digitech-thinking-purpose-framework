### Key ideas
- **Conditional formatting** is a recurring theme — colouring cells based on rules supports maths, literacy and data literacy
- **Fractions scaffold** is a notable resource with video reflection built in — useful for retrieval practice
- **Pixel art** is an engaging hook for introducing spreadsheets to younger students
