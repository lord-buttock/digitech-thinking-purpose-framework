# Panel Review

Run a multi-perspective panel review of a curriculum artefact — a KUD chart, criterion bank, learning target definition, crosswalk, or scope-and-sequence — scoring it from seven reviewer perspectives with a clear pass/fail quality gate. Use when you want a draft framework document stress-tested before sharing it with staff, leadership, or accreditors.
