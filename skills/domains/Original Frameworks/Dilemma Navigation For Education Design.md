# Dilemma Navigation For Education Design

Identify and navigate genuine dilemmas in curriculum, school, or community contexts — tensions between competing goods that cannot be solved, only navigated — producing a structured dilemma map with both poles named and both/and possibilities. Use when a planning conversation keeps circling a tension with no clean answer, like depth vs. coverage or autonomy vs. support.
