# Three Horizons Learning Transition Mapper

Map a scoped topic across three coexisting horizons — the current system under strain, the preferred future, and the transition innovations between them — to understand change dynamics and identify responsible next actions. Use when a class is exploring how a system could change over time, typically after scoping an inquiry.
