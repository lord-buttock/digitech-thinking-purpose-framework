# AI Output Critical Audit Designer

Design a structured protocol for auditing AI-generated text against six critical thinking standards. Use when students need to evaluate the reasoning quality of AI output in any subject. For checking factual accuracy and citations, use ai-hallucination-fact-check-protocol.
