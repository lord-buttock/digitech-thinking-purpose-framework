# Disciplinary AI Literacy Sequence Designer

Design a sequence where students compare AI's handling of the same question across disciplines, developing a mental model of where AI is reliable vs. distorting based on knowledge type. Use when teaching students where AI can and cannot be trusted within and across subject areas.
