# Curriculum Crosswalk

Compare two or more curriculum frameworks and produce a topic matrix showing coverage and gaps across all of them, plus an optional reference-centric crosswalk document for PLC use. Use when comparing a school framework against state standards or mapping overlap between curriculum documents.

## How to invoke

Ask Claude: *"Use the `curriculum-crosswalk` skill to …"*
