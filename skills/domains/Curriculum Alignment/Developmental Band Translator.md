# Developmental Band Translator

Tag decomposed curriculum items (KUDs, learning targets, criteria) with a school's developmental band metadata while preserving the source's voice and labels. Use when re-labelling curriculum content with your school's band system; supply the band schema and the skill derives mapping rules from it.
