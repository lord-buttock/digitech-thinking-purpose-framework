# Coverage Audit

Take a curriculum framework and a statutory or accreditation requirement list and produce a coverage table, gap summary, and CSV showing which framework content covers each requirement and where gaps exist. Use when checking whether a programme covers everything a curriculum authority or accreditor requires.
