# Systems Wellbeing Impact Mapper

Map systemic forces shaping a wellbeing concern without individualising the problem, using Bronfenbrenner's ecological model and social determinants to generate structural interventions. Use when a wellbeing concern like anxiety, disengagement, or bullying needs understanding at whole-school or community level rather than as an individual student problem.
