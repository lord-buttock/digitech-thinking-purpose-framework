# Hinge Question Designer

Design a diagnostic hinge question that reveals whether students understand enough to move on. Use when planning key checkpoints mid-lesson during explicit or direct instruction. For a full protocol of checks across a whole lesson, use checking-for-understanding-protocol-designer.
