# Reflection Activities — Classroom Toolkit

**Source:** [[Education/Teaching Strategies/Reflection-Activities.pdf]]  
**Author:** Samara Abbas (Gamma slides)  
**Purpose:** A bank of structured reflection activities tied to Learning Goals and Success Criteria

---

## Summary

A 12-activity classroom toolkit designed to help students reflect meaningfully on their learning in relation to Learning Goals (LGs) and Success Criteria (SC). Each activity includes sentence starters and is suitable for use at the end of a lesson or learning sequence. Directly supports HITS Strategy 9 (Metacognitive Strategies) and the Ngarri Instructional Model Reflect phase.

---

## Key Points

- All activities are structured around **Learning Goals** and **Success Criteria** — not generic reflection
- Sentence starters are provided for each activity to scaffold student thinking
- Activities range from individual written reflection to whole-class movement-based responses
- Suitable for F–6; most require minimal materials

---

## The 10 Activities

| Activity | Format | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **3-2-1** | Written | 3 things learned, 2 questions, 1 thing to use next time |
| **Glow and Grow** | Written/verbal | What went well vs. what to improve |
| **Finger Rating** | Physical | Rate 1–5 against SC using fingers |
| **SC Sorting** | Card sort / verbal | Sort which SC they've achieved |
| **Quick Interview** | Paired verbal | Students interview each other about their learning |
| **Reflection Ball** | Whole-class movement | Pass a ball; holder shares one reflection |
| **2 Stars and a Wish** | Written | 2 strengths + 1 improvement area |
| **Fist to Chest** | Physical/silent | Confidence check against LG (fist = not yet, open = got it) |
| **Think-Pair-Share** | Paired verbal | Classic structured discussion around LG/SC |
| **Corners** | Movement | Students move to a corner matching their self-assessment level |
| **Emoji Movement Match** | Movement | Students move to match an emoji showing how they feel about their learning |

---

## Connections

- Directly aligns with **HITS Strategy 9: Metacognitive Strategies** and **HITS Strategy 8: Feedback**
- Supports the **Ngarri Instructional Model — Reflect phase** (see [[Education/Teaching Strategies/Ngarri Reflect Phase]])
- Complements **Explain Learning Objectives (AERO)** — once LGs/SC are established, these activities close the loop
- Useful in **coaching conversations** when teachers want practical tools for the reflect phase
